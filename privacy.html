Privacy Policy for Cohesive App

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how Cohesive App ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our application, we require certain permissions and may collect certain information.

Required Permissions

Camera Permission

We request camera access to:

  • Capture documents for text extraction
  • Take photos of handwritten notes
  • Scan physical documents

The photos are processed on your device and optionally uploaded to our secure servers for cloud sync. We never share your photos with third parties.

Data Storage and Security

  • Photos are only taken when you explicitly use the camera feature
  • Images are stored securely and encrypted
  • You can delete your data at any time
  • We don't use photos for any purpose other than providing app functionality
