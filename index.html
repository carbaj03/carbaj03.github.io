Frequently Asked Questions

How is Cohesive different from other note-taking apps?

+

Cohesive is designed specifically for hierarchical thinking. Most note-taking apps focus on linear notes or simple linking. Cohesive's parent-child relationships allow you to organize thoughts in a way that mirrors your natural thinking process, with AI assistance to enhance your ideas.

Is my data private and secure?

+

Yes, your data is private and secure. We use HTTPS for all communication and secure JWT authentication. Your notes are only accessible to you, and our database enforces user-specific data filtering to ensure no one else can access your content. We're continuously improving our security practices.

Can I use Cohesive offline?

+

Currently, Cohesive requires an internet connection to sync your data across devices. We're actively working on offline capabilities for a future release.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

+

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime. After cancellation, you'll continue to have Pro access until the end of your billing period, then you'll automatically move to the Free plan.

Which platforms does Cohesive support?

+

Cohesive is available on iOS, Android, and Web. You can use the same account across all platforms, and your notes will synchronize seamlessly. Our web version works on any desktop browser, and our mobile apps are available in both Apple App Store and Google Play Store.
