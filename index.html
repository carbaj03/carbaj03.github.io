Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cohesive?
Cohesive is an AI-powered note-taking application designed to be your "second brain". It helps you quickly capture thoughts via text, voice, or images, automatically organizes them with tags and links, and uses AI to surface insights and patterns within your notes. It's built with Kotlin Multiplatform to work seamlessly across all your devices.
How does the AI organization work?
When you add notes, Cohesive's AI analyzes the content. It suggests relevant tags based on keywords and context. It also looks for connections between notes, automatically linking related ideas to help you build a web of knowledge and discover relationships you might have missed. Pro users get more advanced analysis and suggestions.
What platforms does Cohesive support?
Cohesive is built using Kotlin Multiplatform, allowing it to run natively on iOS, Android, Web browsers, Windows, macOS, and Linux. Your notes sync across all platforms where you're logged in.
Is my data secure and private?
We take data privacy and security very seriously. Your notes are encrypted in transit and at rest. We utilize industry-standard security practices to protect your information. AI analysis is performed securely, and your personal note content is not used to train models for other users. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for full details.
What are the main differences between Free and Pro plans?
The Free plan provides core note-taking functionality with generous limits on the number of notes, basic tagging, and limited access to AI features (like analysis, suggestions, and voice transcription duration/quantity). The Pro plan offers unlimited notes, storage, tags, reminders, and full access to all current and future advanced AI capabilities with higher limits or unlimited usage, plus priority support. See our Pricing section for a detailed comparison.
Can I import my notes from other applications?
We are actively working on import features for popular note-taking formats and apps like Evernote, Markdown, etc. Please check our roadmap or contact support for the latest status on specific import options.
