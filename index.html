Frequently Asked Questions

How is Cohesive different from other note-taking apps?

+

Cohesive is designed specifically for hierarchical thinking. Most note-taking apps focus on linear notes or simple linking. Cohesive's parent-child relationships allow you to organize thoughts in a way that mirrors your natural thinking process, with AI assistance to enhance your ideas.

Is my data private and secure?

+

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data both in transit and at rest. Your notes are private by default, and we never access your content without explicit permission. You can export your data at any time.

Can I use Cohesive offline?

+

Yes, our mobile and desktop apps support offline usage. Your changes will sync automatically when you reconnect to the internet.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

+

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime. After cancellation, you'll continue to have Pro access until the end of your billing period, then you'll automatically move to the Free plan.
