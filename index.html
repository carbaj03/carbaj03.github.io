Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cohesive?
Cohesive is your external brain. Capture thoughts via voice, text, or photos. AI organizes everything and reveals patterns you'd never notice yourself.
How does AI organization work?
AI analyzes your thoughts and automatically creates relevant tags. It finds connections between notes and builds a knowledge web so you can discover relationships you'd miss.
What platforms work?
iOS, Android, Web, Windows, macOS, and Linux. Your thoughts sync everywhere you go.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your thoughts are encrypted and secure. We use industry-standard practices and never use your personal content to train models for other users.
