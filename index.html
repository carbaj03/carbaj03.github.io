Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Cohesive different from other note-taking apps?
Unlike traditional note-taking apps, Cohesive is designed as your "second brain." It doesn't just store your thoughts—it actively analyzes them to reveal hidden patterns, emotional triggers, and connections you'd never notice on your own. It's the difference between a filing cabinet and a thinking partner.
How does the AI actually understand my thoughts?
Our AI reads context, emotion, and intent—not just keywords. When you write "Another rough meeting with Sarah," it understands this relates to work stress, interpersonal challenges, and recurring patterns. It then connects this to similar entries, tracks emotional trends over time, and can even suggest when you might want to address the underlying issue.
What platforms work?
iOS, Android, Web, Windows, macOS, and Linux. Your thoughts sync everywhere you go.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your thoughts are encrypted and secure. We use industry-standard practices and never use your personal content to train models for other users.
What exactly do I get with the free version?
The free version is genuinely useful—not a teaser. You get 100 thoughts per month (enough for daily journaling), 45-second voice notes, basic AI organization, and 10 smart reminders. Most people find this sufficient to start discovering patterns. Pro removes all limits and adds advanced pattern recognition that reveals deeper insights.
